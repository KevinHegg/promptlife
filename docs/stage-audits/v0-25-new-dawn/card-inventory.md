## 35. Benefits Worth Taking Seriously

- Card id: `benefits-worth-taking-seriously`
- Subtitle: Real help, not utopian hype
- Section/stage: New Dawn
- Path type: ethics
- Stage type: responsible-use
- One-sentence definition: AI benefits are strongest when models amplify human judgment rather than replacing accountability.
- Core explanation: The card separates demonstrated or commonly observed uses, plausible uses, and speculative claims. It names accessibility support, translation, summarization, writing assistance, coding help, document search/RAG, and administrative drafting under human review as bounded uses.
- Where it matters: During deployment and use, when people decide what jobs to give the system and how humans review the output.
- Durable vs temporary: Benefits during use usually come from trained weights plus temporary context, tools, retrieval, and human review. They do not require believing the model is wise.
- Prompt vs response: Good prompts can shape useful context and output format, but the response still needs human judgment.
- Why it matters: A serious view can count real benefits without sliding into hype.
- How it connects: After seeing how human expression powers capability, this card asks what that capability can responsibly help people do.
- Metaphor: A tool garden, not a magic kingdom.
- Brain Bridge: Like a tireless assistant that drafts, searches, translates, and suggests.
- Where the Brain Bridge breaks: The assistant has no judgment, responsibility, or lived understanding.
- Misconception: AI benefits require believing in imminent utopia.
- Glossary chips: accessibility, translation, summarization, RAG, human review, uncertainty, effective prompting.
- Source-needed flags: intentionally cautious; needs task-specific benefit evidence before stronger claims.
- Visual aid id: `benefits-tool-garden`
- Visual type: coded SVG/CSS visual, Zen Garden Map variant.
- Tiny interaction: Current metadata says `Sort benefit strength`, but the visible micro-interaction is still a generic multimodal-style visual action.
- Checkpoint question: Which benefit claim is the safest kind to make without hype?
- Correct answer: AI can assist drafts, search, translation, and access under human review.
