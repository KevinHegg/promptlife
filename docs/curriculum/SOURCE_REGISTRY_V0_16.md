# Source Registry v0.16

Date: 2026-06-05

Purpose: internal source registry for the v0.16 Source Review + Late-Day Copy Discipline pass. This registry supports cautious copy in Midnight Ledger and New Dawn. It is not a learner-facing bibliography and does not add citations to every card.

## Use Rules

- Prefer official frameworks, official laws/regulations, official religious/ethical documents, and primary institutional reports.
- Use reputable news only as secondary orientation.
- Do not publish precise energy, water, labor, copyright, or benefit claims in learner-facing cards unless scoped and source-backed.
- Use source-needed placeholders where evidence remains contested, fast-moving, jurisdiction-specific, or outside current repo context.
- Keep Vatican/Catholic sources in internal review unless the learner-facing card needs them and they are verified.
