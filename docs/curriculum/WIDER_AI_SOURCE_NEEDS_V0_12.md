# Wider AI Source Needs v0.12

Date: 2026-06-04

Purpose: record source-review needs after implementing the v0.12 Wider AI Literacy draft cards. The learner-facing app copy uses cautious language and avoids precise statistics. This document keeps source placeholders internal.

## Source Discipline Used In App Copy

- The app does not publish precise energy, water, carbon, labor, copyright, or benefit statistics.
- Costs language says impacts vary by model, workload, hardware, cooling, region, and electricity source.
- Benefits language separates demonstrated, plausible, and speculative claims without presenting utopian predictions as fact.
- Vatican/Catholic and secular governance frameworks are not quoted in learner copy yet; they remain future source-review candidates.
- No learner-facing UI contains `SOURCE NEEDED`.
