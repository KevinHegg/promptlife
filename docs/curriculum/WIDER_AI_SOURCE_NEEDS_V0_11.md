# Wider AI Source Needs v0.11

Date: 2026-06-04

Purpose: list source-grounding needs before the Wider AI Literacy expansion is implemented or published. Do not invent precise statistics. Use `SOURCE NEEDED: [claim]` placeholders until claims are reviewed against reliable, current sources.

## Environmental Footprint And Infrastructure

- SOURCE NEEDED: Current estimates for AI/data-center environmental footprint, with scope by model type, hardware, region, electricity source, cooling system, and workload.
- SOURCE NEEDED: Energy demand for model training versus inference, with clear dates and measurement boundaries.
- SOURCE NEEDED: Water use in data centers, including differences by cooling method, geography, season, and electricity generation assumptions.
- SOURCE NEEDED: Carbon emissions from AI training and inference, separated from general data-center emissions where possible.
- SOURCE NEEDED: Data center siting impacts on local grids, water systems, communities, and public infrastructure.
- SOURCE NEEDED: Chip supply chain impacts, including mining, manufacturing, packaging, and geopolitical constraints.
- SOURCE NEEDED: E-waste and hardware refresh cycles tied to accelerators and data-center expansion.
